Understanding the Model

This tool uses a log-linear elasticity model:

ΔU (%) = ε_price·ΔP + ε_rebate·ΔR + ε_mls·ΔM + ε_tier·ΔT
Uptake_new = U_baseline + ΔU/100
  • ε_price = –0.30: 1 %↑ premium → 0.30 %↓ uptake
  • ε_rebate = +0.20: 1 %↑ rebate → 0.20 %↑ uptake
  • ε_mls = +0.10: 1 %↑ MLS → 0.10 %↑ uptake
  • ε_tier = +0.15: 1-tier ↑ → 0.15 %↑ uptake

Baseline values:

  • Uptake: 35 %
  • Premium: \$1,200
  • Rebate: 25 %
  • Hospital saving per person: \$400

Use the “Simulator” to see how policy levers shift uptake, costs, and savings.

About PHI Choice Explorer

Purpose: Translate DCE-derived elasticities into intuitive simulations for policymakers, researchers, and the public.

Benefits:

  • Rapid “what-if” analysis of rebate, MLS, premiums, and coverage tiers
  • Clear visualisation of trade-offs and budget impacts
  • Open-source, GitHub-hosted, no back-end required
