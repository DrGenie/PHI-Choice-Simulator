About HIPIS

HIPIS (Health Insurance Policy Interactive Simulator) lets policymakers and analysts explore how changes in premium cost, rebate levels, Medicare Levy Surcharge (MLS), coverage, and provider network access influence PHI uptake, government rebate expenditure, and public hospital savings, both nationally and by state.

How to Use HIPIS

  1. Simulator: Select location, funding model, and adjust policy levers.
  2. Click Run Simulation. Results appear below.
  3. Visualisations: View dynamic, non-overlapping charts of uptake, budget impact, and coefficients.
  4. Scenario Compare: Save multiple scenarios and compare side-by-side.
  5. Save/Export: Download your scenario as CSV or PDF for reports.
